About

My Journey

I made a career jump from a management role in large companies to becoming an all-around creative content generalist.

With a strong background in brand marketing, I now build websites in both WordPress and pure code, create compositions in Photoshop, generate moodboards with AI tools like MidJourney, and edit videos in Premiere Pro.

I embrace AI tools as long as the creative soul behind the prompt makes the final work meaningful. I believe every task—no matter how big or small—deserves love, engagement, and a can-do attitude. That’s where I make a difference.
